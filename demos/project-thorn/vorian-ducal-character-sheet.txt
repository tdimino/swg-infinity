Background:
Denzo is from Nagai, a planet that has waged war against the Tof's for centuries. Denzo is a
respected warrior and soldier of his family. He has fought the Tof's with the undying obsession
of achieving freedom from their powerful enemies, it is for this reason that, like many Nagai, he
is nearly fearless in combat, which could sometimes lead him to reckless leaps into combat of nearly
impossible odds. He is quiet and dark in personality, taking everything around him seriously, those who
manage to gain his comfort, could perhaps hear his dark sense of humour. He is very observant and perceptive.
He seems focused at all times, as if concentrated in a task of utter importance, he does this with speed
but he is very calm and patient.

The Tof invasion has lasted for several centuries and they are still much stronger than the Nagai. Like all
entities with power, they will seek to increase it. They have released an unknown number of scouts to explore the
known galaxy and seek out new conquerable worlds, in which case if they find them: They will be endangered. The Tof's
are an incredible Empire and they can prove an annoyance even to the Empire itself. The Nagai, being an honorable people
would never allow such a threat to linger free without having their hearts know that they have at least attempted to stop
them. In response to the threat, Nagai Intel has sent around a dozen scouts/soldiers that fit the profile to find and assassinate
the Tof units. The problem: finding them. With only his honor, faith and zealous loyalty, Denzo Shiba was one of the dozen
members in the task force...
